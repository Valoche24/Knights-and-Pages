Welcome

Welcome to the KNIGHTS AND PAGES game! In a forest full of danger, there are knights and their servants. An obstacle is facing them, it's up to you to find the solution! This game is a web design project made at ECV

1/3

What is problem to solve?

Many years ago, three knights waited to cross the river neva.Each knight had his own page, so there were six people.

The boat that they had could only carry two.
However the knights were ferocious killers and the three pages were terrified.
In fact, it was certain that any of the pages would die of heart failure if he were not protected at every instant from the other knights by the presence of his own master.

Was there any way to cross everyone over the river
without losing a page ?

2/3

How use the site

The boat has two seats, the boat can't move if it's empty, drag one knight or pages on the boat with a click and a drag with your mouse and click on the button cross the river.Get to the other side, drag your character to the case to deposit them.

Once this window is closed, you can reread the instructions by clicking on the "i" button in the right corner of the game.

challenge yourself with the countdown under the "cross river button".

Good luck!
